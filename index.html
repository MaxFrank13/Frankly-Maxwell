Who am I?

A front-end developer based out of Southern Maine. I like to make a product that is simple and interactive while adding a unique artistic flair.

Building and problem solving are inherent to who I am. Prior to working in web development, I was building high-end furniture in workshops. That experience shaped my development style and continues to have a positive influence.

I look to give clients a clean product that satisfies their needs while providing cordial and prompt service.
